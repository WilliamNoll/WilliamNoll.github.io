Research

I'm interested in neural engineering, motor control, and implantable electronics (brain-computer interfaces (BCIs) used for NeuroProstheses, spinal cord injury, motor intent/speech decoding). I have side interests in signal processing and statistics. My long term vision is to streamline BCIs to treat neuromuscular disorders, as well as leveraging BCIs to ask basic neuroscience questions. My dream is to be on the frontline of neurotechnology research and development to improve the quality of life of those around me. I plan to pursue graduate school and a PhD. For fun, I enjoy going to the gym and hiking, as well as making corny jokes to my peers.
