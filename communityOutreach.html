Community Outreach

  • President, NSF-funded B.R.A.I.N. Center

    • President of student group for an Industry–University Cooperative Research Centers (IUCRC) program that accelerates the impact of basic research through close relationships between industry innovators, world-class academic teams, and government leaders.

    • Founded a mentorship program that connects first-generation students to ASU neural engineering faculty or neurotechnology industry members to facilitate workforce development.

    • Pairing first-generation high schoolers with ASU students to assist with college and scholarship essays.

    • Creating interactive neural engineering demos to introduce the youth to foundational neuroscience.
